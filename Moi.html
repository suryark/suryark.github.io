More about Me

I was born and grew up in Muscat, Oman. I have spent the bulk of my life in the Middle East. Growing up I was always involved in sports and extra curriculars.

I play Basketball, Soccer, Cricket, Squash and a variety of other sports including combat sports. I trained for 7 years in Shorin Ryu Karate earning a Dan 2 Black Belt and occasionally do Muay Thai in university.

I have a deep interest in music, and learnt Indian classical music from my grandmother and my mother. I play the guitar as well, and have performed multiple times on stage.

I have been privelleged enough to have travelled extensively so early in my life. Since a very early age, my parents took me to various places around the world, including Kenya, all over Europe, the USA, and other countries in South East Asia. When I was in 6th and 8th grade, I took part in expeditions from school into the Himalayas, spending more than a week trekking up the mountains to glaciers 15,000ft high.

In 11th grade, I took an opportunity in school to do an exchange in South Africa, at the Tiger Kloof Educational Institute in Vryburg, SA. The cultural shock took a while to get over, and living alone in a rural part of an unknown country took a while to get used to, but the experience really taught me a lot.

Research & Projects

HUAT

For my final year Capstone project, I am part of a team, working with local fintech startup Smartkarma, involved in the development of an online, and automated financial advisory. We intend to use the scalability provided by online services to bring smart, cost effective and reliable portfolio rebalancing and financial advice to the average Singaporean. This project is currently ongoing and you can find out more here

Operations Management Project

Between January and May 2015, I was part of a team that worked on an inventory management project with Penguin Books Singapore. We analysed the inventory management system currently in use at Penguin's warehouse, to identify operations inefficiencies. After a thorough analysis, and a deeper look into their value chain, we developed a new inventory management system to prevent stockouts and maintain customer satisfaction, while ensuring costs were minimised.

Network Modelling Project

Between March and May 2015, a team mate and I collaborated with dutch startup Chordify, to visualise and analyse a network of chords used in major songs. We were able to validate a hypothesis in music theory, that most chord patterns follow a certain structure, through network analysis.

Engineering Systems Project

Between September and December 2014, I was part of a team that undertook a material stocking, assignment and inventory management project for the SUTD Architectural studio. We first analysed and forecasted the demand for material from the faculty and students. We then developed a inventory management algorithm for the resident stock keepers to help them decide when and how much to order, and how to allocate and store these materials.

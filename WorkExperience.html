Work Experience

Apple Inc.

Over the Summer of 2014, I did a 14-week internship at Apple Inc. in Singapore, as a WW Test Engineering Intern. My task over the period of the internship was to develop an application to automate the calculation of Gage Reproducibility and Repeatability (GR&R) tests, to be used on factory floors to speed up this key Quality Test. The calculation of GR&R is a key component of the Six Sigma methodology of quality assurance.

Calculation of the GR&R value is done using the statistical ethod known as the Analysis of Variance (ANOVA). The project scope was expanded midway through the timeline, but the requirements were met at the end of the 14 weeks.

The application was developed using Python and Cocoa. It simplified the calculation process and reduced the duration from 4 mins per file, to a matter of seconds per file (~48x faster).

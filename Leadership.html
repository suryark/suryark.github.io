Leadership

Senior Academic Senator

For my senior year at SUTD I was Academic Senator for the ESD pillar, sitting in on the main Legislative student body, the Student Association Council. The Responsibilities of this post was twofold.

The first responsibility, to the pillar, included the constant feedback to pillar administration and the Head of the Pillar on the structure of the course, based on comments from my peers. I also had to spearhead the organisation of cohesion events and other intra piller events.

The second responsibility, to the university, included the analysis and deconstruction of motions brought forward, and vote on these issues along with the other Senators on the council. Topics for decision, ranged from things such as the procedures for setting up societies, to the allocation of rooms in the hostel, all with the welfare of the student population in mind

Student Initiatives Executive

As part of the 2nd Student Government of SUTD from January 2014 to January 2015, I was involved in inspiring and facilitating the setting up and running of close to 60 student run clubs. These clubs are the crucial extra curricular outlets for our students. As the Student Initiatives Executive, I was also involved in the decision making process, for the allocation of funds to these clubs, lobbying on behalf of the clubs.

SUTD Soccer President

For the year 2015, I served as the President of the SUTD Soccer Club. My responsibilities included the organising of training sessions with the coach and friendly games, and the allocation of club funds towards equipment, coaching fees, and enrichment events.

Treasurer of SUTD Basketball

For the year 2014, I served as the Treasurer of the SUTD Basketball Club, where I was in charge of managing and planning the expenditure of a budget of $3,000, for coaching, equipment and events
